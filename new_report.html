Methodology

Contributions are modelled monthly and escalated annually at the stated contribution escalation rate, shown here as 6.00% p.a. Retirement and discretionary balances compound at the selected nominal return, shown here as 7.50% p.a.

Values marked as today's money are deflated using the CPI assumption, shown here as 5.00% p.a. Capital events are included where they have been captured in the calculator.

The projected starting retirement income applies an age-based safe withdrawal rate to the combined real capital at retirement and divides the result by twelve. The safe withdrawal rate shown on the projection page is the rate used for the selected retirement age.
