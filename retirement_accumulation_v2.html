Methodology & disclaimer

Methodology & assumptions

Notes on methodology. Contributions are made monthly at the stated amounts, with the monthly amount escalating once per year by the annual escalation rate. The retirement fund and discretionary balances grow at the stated nominal return, compounded monthly. Values labelled "today's money" are deflated by the CPI rate to express future amounts in current purchasing power. Projected monthly retirement income applies an age-based safe withdrawal rate to the combined capital (retirement fund plus discretionary), divided by twelve, shown in today's money and before tax. The rate is read from a schedule that rises with the age at retirement (a shorter remaining horizon supports a higher sustainable draw), ranging from about 4.2% at age 55 to 4.8% at age 65 and higher at older ages. This rule-of-thumb figure is appropriate for early-stage planning conversations; a more detailed drawdown projection with tax and regulatory constraints should be produced closer to the client's retirement date. No allowance has been made for tax on contributions or on investment income, platform fees, advice fees, retirement fund Section 10C deductions, or changes in contribution patterns over the working career.
